Recipe for Classic Casserole

Description

A casserole is both a type of large, deep pan or bowl that is used to cook a range of dishes in the oven .

Ingredients

  • 1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 2 cans green beans
  • 1 1/3 cups french fried onions
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Add mushroom soup, milk, black pepper, green beans, 2/3 cup of fried onions in bowl
  2. Bake at 350Â°F for 25 minutes after pouring it in a 1 1/2 quart casserole pan.
