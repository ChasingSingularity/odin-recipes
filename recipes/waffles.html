Recipe for Classic Waffles

Description

A waffle is made of leavened dough and cooking between two plates gives a unique impression of size, shape and surface. There are many variations depending on the type of waffle iron used. Waffles are eaten all over the world, especially in Belgium, where there are more than a dozen variety. Waffles can be fresh or reheated after commercial baking.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 Tbsp. sugar
  • eggs
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch

Steps

  1. Mix wet ingredients
  2. Mix corn starch, flour, salt, sugar, baking powder and baking soda in a bowl
  3. Combine dry and wet ingredients
  4. Cook in a waffle iron
