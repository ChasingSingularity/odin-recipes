Recipe for Classic Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the earliest form of pasta formed of wide, flat sheets. It consists of lasagne sheets layered on top of one another and alternated with ingredients such as meats, vegetables, cheeses, herbs, and spices.Usually, the assembled ingredients including the cooked pasta are baked in the oven after assembly.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 160 grams onions
  • 1 Tbsp. garlic
  • 454 grams beef
  • 454 grams sausage meat
  • 0.79 kilograms tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp. tomato paste
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. black pepper
  • 1 tsp. oregano
  • 2 Tbsp. basil
  • 1 pinch red pepper
  • 2 tsp. mint
  • 0.91 kilograms ricotta cheese
  • 112 grams mozzarella
  • 2 eggs
  • 100 grams parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 30 grams parsley
  • 0.59 liters tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna lightly .
  2. Sauté onion over medium heat for two min. Add garlic, meat, tomatoe, tomato puree, salt, pepper, oregano, basil, red pepper and mint. Place over medium heat for ten min.
  3. Combine ricotta, mozzarella, beaten egg, parmesan, salt, pepper and parsley.
  4. Pour tomato sauce in the bottom and place four lasagna noodles. Add one third of the cheese, one third of the meat and half mozzarella cheese. Use four more noodles, 1/3 of the cheese , 1/3 of the meat and mozzarella. Top with four more noodles, cover with remaining cheese, remaining meat and mozzarella cheese. Four more noodles at the end, 1½ cups tomato sauce, 1½ cup shredded mozzarella and ½ cup grated parmesan.
  5. Place a sheet of parchment paper on top and cover tightly with aluminum foil.
  6. Cook for one hour then bake uncovered for 30 min.
  7. Cut 3X4 for 12 pieces.
